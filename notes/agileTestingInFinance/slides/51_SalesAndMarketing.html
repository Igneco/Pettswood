Sales and Marketing

Common Approachs

  • The Consultant: "This is just how we work."
  • The Credit Crunch Consultant: "I'll do it your way."
  • The Fait Accompli: "See, it just works" (after two months of covert hackery)
  • Enlist a Deputy: sell the idea to someone else, then get them to present it. You can wade in and answer the harder questions.
  • Divide and Conquer: never introduce an idea to many people at once. Spend time with each person, sounding out their concerns, then make sure you have an answer for each concern.
  • The Alpha Course: Send 'em to a conference or training course.
